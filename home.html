Welcome to Soar 2019!

Suddenly Opportunities are Reachable (SOAR) is a leadership conference which focuses on helping middle school students effectively transition into high school.

WHAT IS SOAR?

Delegates attending SOAR will receive valuable insight and advice about high school life, all while participating in fun leadership activities and workshops. In addition, they will learn about extra curricular opportunities they can pursue throughout their high school years, and meet motivational speakers to inspire them to become involved in the community. The day is full of food, fun, and dancing.

Join us on Saturday, June 1st at Turner Fenton Secondary School to learn to how you can make your high school experience as amazing as possible!
